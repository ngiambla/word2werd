Word2Werd

If you replace the % and # in **%*#** with ^ and >, then you can form a new word, **^*>**

If this sounds like something you've encountered before, now there's a game with for it. Here are the rules:

  1. You are provided with a word of N-Characters
  2. You are to form a new word of the same size
  3. You can use any characters of the English alphabet to form the new word
  4. The new word cannot be the provided word!
Scoring:
  • Less frequently used words provide a higher score (i.e., grapple is higher in value than through)1
  • Every new character in the new word quarters your score
  • An invalid word (i.e., a word that doesn't actually exist) halves your score (once you have a successful submission)
